How to Download Your Aadhaar Card

  1. Click on the "Download Aadhaar" button below
  2. You will be redirected to the official UIDAI website
  3. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar Number or 16-digit Virtual ID
  4. Provide the security code and request OTP
  5. Enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number
  6. Download your Aadhaar card in PDF format
