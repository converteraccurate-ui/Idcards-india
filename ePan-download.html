How to Get Your Instant e-PAN

  1. Click on the "Get Instant e-PAN" button below
  2. You will be redirected to the official Income Tax e-Filing portal
  3. Select the 'Instant e-PAN' service from the options
  4. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar Number
  5. Provide the security code and request OTP
  6. Enter the OTP received on your Aadhaar-registered mobile number
  7. Validate your details and download your e-PAN

Eligibility for Instant e-PAN

  • You must be an Indian citizen
  • You should not already have a PAN card
  • Your mobile number must be registered with Aadhaar
  • Your complete date of birth should be available in Aadhaar
  • You should have a valid email address

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Instant e-PAN?
Instant e-PAN is a facility provided by the Income Tax Department that allows eligible individuals to get their PAN instantly in electronic form (e-PAN) through Aadhaar-based e-KYC, without submitting any physical application or documents.
Is e-PAN equivalent to physical PAN card?
Yes, e-PAN is a valid proof of PAN and is equivalent to the physical PAN card. It can be used for all official purposes where PAN is required. The e-PAN is a digitally signed document by the issuing authority.
What if I already have a PAN card?
The Instant e-PAN service is only for individuals who do not have a PAN card. If you already have a PAN, you can use the e-PAN facility to download a digital copy of your existing PAN.
Is there any fee for Instant e-PAN?
No, the Instant e-PAN service is completely free of charge. You don't need to pay any fees to get your e-PAN through this Aadhaar-based process.
