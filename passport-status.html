Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my passport application status?
You can track your passport application status using your file number and date of birth on the official Passport Seva website. Alternatively, you can call the Passport Seva helpline or visit the nearest Passport Seva Kendra.
What is the typical processing time for a passport?
Normal passport applications typically take 30-45 days for processing, while Tatkaal applications are processed within 7-14 days. These timelines may vary based on police verification and other factors.
What should I do if my application is delayed?
If your application is delayed beyond the expected timeline, you can contact the Passport Seva helpline, send an email to the Regional Passport Office, or visit the Passport Seva Kendra where you applied for assistance.
