Frequently Asked Questions

What is e-EPIC?
e-EPIC is a portable document format (PDF) version of the Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC) that can be downloaded on a mobile or computer. It is a secure, non-editable PDF with a digital signature.
Is e-EPIC a valid proof of identity?
Yes, the e-EPIC is a valid proof of identity and can be used for various purposes where identity verification is required, subject to acceptance by the requesting entity.
What if I don't have a registered mobile number?
You need a mobile number registered with your voter ID to receive OTP for downloading e-EPIC. If your mobile number is not registered, you can visit the nearest voter service center to update it.
